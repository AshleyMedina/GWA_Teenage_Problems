Sex Education

An uncomfortable topic

Often the uncomfortable nature of talking about sex can cause people to forgo discussion entirely. Teenagers that are not encouraged to discuss this topic openly might not search out the information for themselves. However, an ignorance about sex can not only make one feel isolated and uncomfortable with their sexuality, but it can be dangerous as well. To start researching more about sex and sexuality, check out the links below.
