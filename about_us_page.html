About Us

GWA(Girls With Attitude), formed in the summer of 2016,
is composed of students from the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program.
As young women coming into the tech industry, GWA produced a website to inform
teenagers about the importance of emotional, physical and mental health.
This website,"Being A Teenager", helps teens from everywhere overcome
stress, sadness and anger with words of encouragement,
entertaining videos, informative facts and more.
